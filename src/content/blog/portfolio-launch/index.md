# Building a Data Science Portfolio

This portfolio is the home for my data science work as I prepare for my next role.

The site will collect project case studies, notebook-backed analysis, writing about methods and tradeoffs, and a downloadable resume.

## What belongs here

- Practical data science projects with clear problem statements.
- Exploratory analysis and modelling notes converted from Jupyter notebooks.
- Short posts about lessons learned, tools, and communication.
- Resume and contact links for hiring teams.

## Writing principle

Each project should explain the decision context, the data, the method, the result, and what I would improve next.
